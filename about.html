About Me

Allow me to introduce myself formally. My name is Ke’Mari, but I commonly go by Key—I’ll pause for your “Aha!” moment—and I am a multimedia artist and designer with an aptitude for creative writing. I’ve been a prodigy of the creative arts and technology since childhood. From writing short stories and drawing comic books in grade school to building my first website at age 12 to getting my first DSLR camera and learning graphic design at 16, every step has developed me into the artist and writer I am today. My main drive is to reveal the divine beauty hidden in our sometimes vapid world with a bit of imagination and appreciation. If you wish to discover how to unlock this potential in your work-life, reach out to me, and I’ll offer you the key.
