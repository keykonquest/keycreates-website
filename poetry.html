Man on Fire

I'm the man on fire.
You'll see my sparks
Arc across the dark
Gasoline pumps through my heart
My thoughts are like embers
When they fly from my mind
My spine is a fuse that's low on time
In the blink of an eye,
I'll up and combust
So no wonder why everything I touch
Turns to dust
These streams of steam
Flowing from my eyes
Come from a flame inside
That never dies
So it's no wonder all my
bridges are burnt
Quite frankly,
I'd be surprised if they weren't
I ignite every item
That comes in my path
Everything I touch turns to ash
I'm an eternal inferno of angst
Surrounded by propane tanks
So to intimacy, I say "No thanks"
As my life goes up in smoke.

I say "I'm okay. Don't be concerned,
But don't stand too close
cause you might get burned!"
I'm the man on fire.
I'm wrapped in a flame
No fabric can retard
My hair has been singed
And I'm horribly scarred
So don't let down your guard
And nobody gets hurt
I admit that it's hard,
But maybe there's worse
Than to be burdened
With this lonely curse,
So I stumble this Earth
In search of some help
Hoping the answer isn't
To extinguish myself.

The Wisdom of Trees

Oh green trees
May your leaves offer me the key
Cause when I look around
Let me tell you what I see
Generations of slaves,
Aching to be freed
And the masters of deceit
Telling them that all they need
Is to keep grinding away
Then happiness is guaranteed
Please.
You think that will make you complete?
When all you do is consume,
Work, sleep, then repeat
Its a thick sheet of lies
But let's see what lies beneath

Oh green trees
May your leaves offer me relief
I live in a space
Where there's faith,
But not enough belief
Without a shred of love, only grief
I can harldy sleep
I'm searching for an answer
It's hidden in there deep
Under the myths they preach
The narcissism that they teach

Cause eveybody's saying
"Hey world, look at me!"
But if we all looked inside
'Wonder where we could be
If you looked inside,
Would you like what you see?

Oh green trees
May your leaves offer me some peace
Because I know a humble man
With humble thoughts is obsolete
You're supposed to want more
You're not supposed to be content
Until you're old and gray
Asking where the time went
There's nothing wrong with dreams
But what does that dream represent?
Is it really even yours?
Or is it one that you were lent?
Is your mind up for sale?
Is your time up for rent?
If you weren't chasing money
How would your time be spent?
I just hope it's not too late
For us to wake up and repent
And grow like the green trees.

Fallen Angels
Fallen angels can't fall asleep
Because their minds are never at ease
The world's too shallow
And evil runs deep
So fallen angels can't fall asleep

Fallen angels can't fall in love
Because water and oil don't mix
What's pure will stay down
The other above
So fallen angels can't fall in love

Fallen angels can't fall in line
Thier colors glow too bold to subdue
In a world filled with darkness
They always shine
So fallen angels can't fall in line

Fallen angels can't fall back
They are soldiers of the forsaken
It's a war for our souls
The enemy's on the attack
So fallen angels can't fall back

And should a fallen angel
Ever fall apart
And become just
Another broken heart
An agent of apathy
A blessing turned bitter
So sheds another minute on the clock
Another tear drop from the sky
For the end is nigh
The final chapter of this tragedy
All the angels are in agony
Searching for salvation, a saving grace
Perhaps they're here by mistake
Or sacrificed in the name of fate
For the night's moon
Brings haunting mystery
But heaven's revealed
In the morning light
But the night is cold, so very cold
And so dark, so very dark
In the black hides unbearable pain
And predators thirsty for warm blood
There's many wolves
Disguised as sheep
So fallen angels can't fall asleep
